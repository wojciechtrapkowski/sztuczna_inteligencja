In the West Bank and Gaza Strip Israeli soldiers shot and killed three Palestinians today as Israel tightened its blockade of Palestinian cities .
Officials said that closure was punishment for yesterday 's drive - by shootings that killed four Israelis .
NPR 's Jennifer Loden reports .
In addition to not being allowed to enter Israel , Palestinians are not permitted to travel between the West Bank cities now .
Such collective punishment is illegal under Geneva conventions .
Israeli officials say Palestinians can cross checkpoints for humanitarian reasons , but in one village near Ramallah residents said a 34 - year - old man died after Israeli troops refused to let him go to a hospital .
The man had a heart condition and had inhaled tear gas .
Israeli media said the Army is considering further travel restriction for Palestinians in an attempt to prevent more drive - by shootings .
One official calls the new phase of the conflict , the war of the roads .
Palestinians warned that Wednesday , they 'll attempt to block the roads to Jewish settlements .
Jennifer Loden , NPR News , Jerusalem .
