President Clinton has invited Palestinian leader Yasser Arafat to a meeting in Washington .
November Arafat tells CNN he hopes to go , but the date may conflict with a planned event at the upcoming Islamic countries summit in Qatar .
Mr. Clinton has not yet invited Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Barak , but a spokesman for Barak says if he 's invited , the Prime Minister would go to Washington a couple of days after Arafat .
